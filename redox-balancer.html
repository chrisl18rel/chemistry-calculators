TIPS
• State symbols are required — (aq) compounds are split into ions; (s), (l), (g) stay as formula units
• Solubility rules and weak acid/base rules are applied automatically
• Spectator ions are identified and cancelled to give the net ionic equation
• Oxidation numbers are assigned automatically — fractional states supported
• Steps follow the half-reaction method for acidic or basic solutions
• For very complex multi-element redox, results may need manual verification
