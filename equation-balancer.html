TIPS
• Paste directly from a worksheet — blanks like ___ or ____ are stripped automatically
• State symbols (s), (l), (g), (aq) are ignored
• Existing coefficients are removed before balancing
• Use any arrow: →, 🡪, =>, or ->
• Separate compounds with +
• Use parentheses for polyatomic ions: Ca(OH)2
• Use * for hydrates: CuSO4*5H2O
• For redox equations use the Redox Balancer tab
