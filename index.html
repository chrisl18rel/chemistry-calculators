HOW TO ENTER IONS
Always use curly braces for charges:

I3{-1}triiodide, I3−
S2O3{2-}thiosulfate, S2O32−
MnO4{-}permanganate, MnO4−
Fe{2+}iron(II) ion, Fe2+
Cr2O7{2-}dichromate, Cr2O72−
NH4{+}ammonium, NH4+

Neutral compounds like CuSO4, Fe, H2O need no braces.

• Solubility rules auto-applied to molecular (aq) equations
• Spectator ions cancelled automatically
• Fractional oxidation states supported (e.g. I3− = −⅓)
